What is EmoBot?

EmoBot is a tool designed for the CUTIE Interviewer Bot.


How does it work?

It has 2 main features:

  • facial recognition
  • text recognition

The EmoBot uses facial recognition to detect these following features of the user during the course of the interview stimulation:

  • facial expressions
  • mood, and
  • attentiveness
    • When using the Interviewer bot, you get to choose your interviewer

    • then you confirm your selection

    • and then you proceed with your interview with your chosen interviewer

With this, the CUTIE interviewer bot can appropriately respond to what the interviewee is currently feeling and or experiencing such as:

  • being too anxious, or
  • being too giddy

This will also alert the bot if the user is: as they would have during real job interviews.

  • paying attention, or
  • taking the interviews seriously

Lastly, the EmoBot uses text recognition to:

  • guess or analyze the user's emotions and mood based on the way the interviewee replies to the bot.

For example:

  • the bot would assume that the interviewee is happy to conduct the interview
    • if the user starts the conversation with a Good Morning or a Good Afternoon greeting.
  • The bot will also reply with a suggestion or a pat-in-the back message if the user asks for a tip or if the user feels down or discouraged.

Who would benefit from this?

This application will greatly benefit most especially the graduating college students of Cebu Institute of Technology University because:

  • they can now be sure that they would ace their future job interviews with ease and confidence.

With the help of this application in partnership with the CUTIE interviewer bot:

  • the number of hired Cebu Institute of Technology University fresh graduates will surely increase.
