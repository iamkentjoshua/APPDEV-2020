What is EmoBot?

EmoBot is a tool designed for the CUTIE Interviewer Bot.


How does it work?

It has 2 main features, facial recognition and text recognition.

The EmoBot uses facial recognition to detect the user's facial expressions, mood,
and attentiveness during the course of the interview simulation.
With this, the CUTIE interviewer bot can appropriately respond to what the interviewee
is currently feeling and or experiencing such as being too anxious or too giddy.
This will also alert the bot if the user is paying attention or taking
the interviews seriously as they would have during real job interviews.

Lastly, the EmoBot uses text recognition to guess or analyze the user's emotions
and mood based on the way the interviewee replies to the bot.
For example, the bot would assume that the interviewee is happy to conduct the interview
if the user starts the conversation with a Good Morning or a Good Afternoon greeting.
The bot will also reply with a suggestion or a pat-in-the back message
if the user asks for a tip or if the user feels down or discouraged.


Who would benefit from this?

This application will greatly benefit most especially the graduating college students of
Cebu Institute of Technology University because they can now be sure that
they would ace their future job interviews with ease and confidence.
With the help of this application in partnership with the CUTIE interviewer bot,
the number of hired Cebu Institute of Technology University fresh graduates will surely increase.
